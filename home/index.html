CAR UI is an initiative of UNITiD interaction designers

Digital interactive displays are popping up in all new produced cars. And with this trend, new problems of interaction arrise, that were long covered in the era of fysical buttons and sliders. With our experions in digital design we believed that it was time to pinpoint those problems, and started our CAR UI initiative.
